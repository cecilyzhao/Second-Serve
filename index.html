Why Food Waste Matters

Every day, perfectly edible food is thrown away while millions of people struggle with food insecurity.

Food waste also contributes heavily to greenhouse gas emissions and environmental damage.

Second Serve aims to change how people think about food and consumption.

About Our Project

Second Serve is a student-led initiative focused on reducing food waste in households, schools, and local communities.

Every year, a large amount of edible food is wasted while many people face food insecurity.

Our goal is to promote awareness and practical solutions to reduce waste.

Together, We Can Make a Difference

Reducing food waste protects our planet and supports communities worldwide.
